TaskFlow Billing and Subscription Policies
==========================================

Pricing Plans
-------------

FREE PLAN ($0/month)
- Up to 3 active projects
- Up to 5 team members
- 1GB file storage
- Basic task management
- Email support (48-hour response)

PREMIUM PLAN ($15/user/month, billed annually)
- Unlimited projects
- Unlimited team members
- 100GB file storage per team
- Advanced features: Timeline view, Custom fields, Automations
- Priority email support (24-hour response)
- Team analytics and reporting

ENTERPRISE PLAN ($30/user/month, billed annually)
- Everything in Premium plus:
- 500GB file storage per team
- Single Sign-On (SSO) with SAML 2.0
- Advanced security controls
- Custom integrations via API
- Dedicated account manager
- Priority phone and chat support (4-hour response)
- 99.9% SLA uptime guarantee

Payment Information
-------------------

Accepted Payment Methods:
- Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover
- PayPal
- Wire transfer (Enterprise annual plans only)

Billing Cycle:
- Monthly plans are billed on the same date each month
- Annual plans are billed once per year with 20% discount
- Enterprise contracts can have custom billing arrangements

How to Update Payment Method:
1. Go to Settings > Billing > Payment Methods
2. Click "Add Payment Method"
3. Enter your new card details
4. Set as default payment method
5. Remove old payment method if desired

Upgrading Your Plan
-------------------

Steps to Upgrade:
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Click "Change Plan"
3. Select your desired plan
4. Review prorated charges
5. Confirm upgrade

Prorated Billing:
When upgrading mid-cycle, you'll only be charged the prorated difference for the remaining days in your billing period.

Downgrading Your Plan
---------------------

Steps to Downgrade:
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Click "Change Plan"
3. Select a lower-tier plan
4. Acknowledge feature limitations
5. Confirm downgrade

Important Notes:
- Downgrade takes effect at the end of current billing period
- You keep access to premium features until then
- Excess data beyond new plan limits must be removed before downgrade

Cancellation Policy
-------------------

How to Cancel:
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Complete the cancellation survey (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation

What Happens After Cancellation:
- Access continues until end of paid period
- After that, account converts to Free plan
- Data is retained for 30 days
- After 30 days, data is permanently deleted
- You can reactivate within 30 days to restore data

Refund Policy
-------------

14-Day Money-Back Guarantee:
- Full refund available within 14 days of initial purchase
- Applies to first-time Premium and Enterprise subscriptions
- Does not apply to renewals or add-ons

How to Request a Refund:
1. Contact support@taskflow.com
2. Include your account email and reason for refund
3. Refunds processed within 5-7 business days

Non-Refundable Items:
- Subscriptions older than 14 days
- Renewal charges (cancel before renewal date)
- Custom development or consulting services
- Enterprise contracts (separate terms apply)

Invoice and Tax Information
---------------------------

Accessing Invoices:
- Go to Settings > Billing > Invoice History
- Download PDF invoices for any billing period
- Invoices are also emailed upon payment

Tax Information:
- Prices shown are exclusive of taxes unless stated
- VAT/GST applied based on your location
- Tax-exempt organizations can upload exemption certificate in Billing settings
- W-9 available upon request for US organizations
